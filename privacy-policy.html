Privacy Policy for YouTube Sampler

Last Updated: March 30, 2026

Overview

YouTube Sampler ("we", "our", or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle data when you use our Chrome extension.

Data We Do NOT Collect

YouTube Sampler does NOT collect, store, transmit, or share any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, address)
  • Browsing history or web activity
  • Location data
  • Audio or video content from YouTube
  • YouTube account information
  • Search queries or watch history
  • Any personally identifiable information

Data Stored Locally

The extension stores minimal data locally on your device using Chrome's storage API:

  • Export Count: A number tracking how many times you've exported audio (used only for free tier limitation of 3 exports)
  • User Preferences: Your last used settings for effects (reverb level, delay time, etc.)

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any servers.

Audio Processing

All audio processing happens entirely in your browser using the Web Audio API:

  • Audio is processed in real-time locally on your device
  • No audio data is uploaded, transmitted, or stored on any servers
  • When you record, the audio is saved directly to your device's download folder
  • We have no access to the audio you process or record

Third-Party Services

ExtensionPay (Payment Processing)

When you choose to upgrade to the PRO plan, the extension uses ExtensionPay for secure payment processing:

  • ExtensionPay uses Stripe for payment processing
  • We do NOT have access to your payment card information
  • ExtensionPay collects only information necessary for payment processing
  • Your payment data is handled according to ExtensionPay's Privacy Policy and Stripe's Privacy Policy

The extension only receives a "paid" or "unpaid" status from ExtensionPay - no payment details are shared with us.

Permissions Explained

activeTab

Required to insert audio processing controls into YouTube video pages. This permission allows the extension to add UI elements and access the video element for audio processing.

storage

Required to save your export count and preferences locally on your device.

extensionpay.com

Required to communicate with ExtensionPay's payment processing service for PRO plan upgrades.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with anyone.

The only data interaction with third parties is:

  • Payment processing through ExtensionPay/Stripe (only when you choose to upgrade)

Children's Privacy

YouTube Sampler is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. You can:

  • Clear your local preferences by uninstalling the extension
  • Reset your export count by clearing Chrome's extension storage

Contact

For privacy-related questions or concerns, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store support page for YouTube Sampler.
